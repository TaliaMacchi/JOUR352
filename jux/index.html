Taylor Swift

The American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift began singing at age 10, performing at local fairs, sporting events and contests. She started playing guitar and writing songs at age 12. In 2006, at age 16, she released her first single, “Teardrops on My Guitar.” Twenty years later, she is 36 and still doing what she loves successfully. She has released 12 original albums and four re-recorded albums, gone on six world tours and won 14 Grammy Awards.
