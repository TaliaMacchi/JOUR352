Why I Love Gracie Abrams

I have been a fan of Gracie Abrams since her EPs first came out, so you could definitely call me a superfan. I was so excited when I realized she was opening for Taylor Swift and would be performing at the show I attended. That moment made me love her music even more and listen to it more often. Her sound is soft yet raw, with simple instrumentation that allows her strong vocals to stand out. Her lyricism is also incredibly compelling — she captures relatable experiences like young love, heartbreak and situationships in a way that feels honest and emotional. Her newest album has no skips, and I love the deluxe version even more. I can’t wait to see what she does with her next project.
